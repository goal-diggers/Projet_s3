Beyond The Words

The objective of this project is to develop OCR (Optical Character Recognition) software capable of solving a word search puzzle. On this platform, you’ll be able to view the images before and after the preprocessing has been applied, highlighting the steps and techniques we used to transform them. We hope this presentation will help you better understand the challenges we’ve tackled and the innovative solutions we’ve brought to our project.

Enjoy your exploration!
